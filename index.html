About Me

My name is Renata Dickinson and I am a software developer (in training) living in Fort Worth, Texas.

I am originally from Brazil and I used to work as an English teacher there. I have a Bachelor's and a Licenciate Degree in History. My most recent job is as a barista at Starbucks, which is my current one.

I wanted to start working as a programmer because, as Mechatronic Engineering student dropout, I enjoyed my programming classes. It is time to give programming another shot!

As for my free time, I love playing video-games (mostly World of Warcraft), having fun with some VR, music, biking, reading (mostly History books). Absolutely adore chocolate and the sweet and sour combination!

I am also very outgoing, but at the same time love to stay at home.
